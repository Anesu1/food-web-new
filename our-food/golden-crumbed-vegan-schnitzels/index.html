Golden Crumbed Schnitzels

Oh my Schnitzel! Our favourite Schnitzeys are almost too good to be true. How can we be so sure? Well, they are meat-free and egg-free, crunchy, crispy and golden on the outside, and tender on the inside. Choose to braai, grill, fry or bake them or even pop them in the toaster! Serve with lightly steamed vegetables, sticky rice and a squeeze of lemon zest for a high protein meal.

Ingredients

Crumb: Wheat Flour, Salt, Sunflower Oil. Schnitzel: Vegetable Protein (Soya, Wheat), Flavourings (Savoury Flavour, Pepper, Onion, Citric Acid, Anti-caking Agent [E341]), Potato Starch, Garlic, Rosemary, Marjoram, Sage, Mustard, Maize Starch, Thickener (Methyl Cellulose), Ground Coriander (Sulphites), Onion, Salt, Plant Fibre, Colours: Caramel IV, Red Iron Oxide

Allergens

Wheat Gluten Soya Mustard Sulphites
